# 7D Agile × SWEET Principles Mapping

This table shows how the SWEET Agile principles (Simplicity, Workable, Empirical, Empowering, Transparent) align with each of the 7D Agile lifecycle phases.

| 7D Phase       | Simplicity | Workable | Empirical | Empowering | Transparent |
|----------------|------------|----------|-----------|------------|-------------|
| **Discover**   | Focus on a clear vision; avoid overcomplicating early goals. | Ensure ideas are practical and achievable. | Validate assumptions with quick research or prototypes. | Involve stakeholders early to shape direction. | Share vision openly with all team members. |
| **Define**     | Keep requirements lean; avoid bloated specs. | Prioritize feasible scope over “wish lists.” | Use data from past projects to refine scope. | Let teams co-create backlog items. | Make scope and constraints visible to all. |
| **Design**     | Favor simple architectures and UX flows. | Ensure designs can be implemented within sprint limits. | Test design choices with user feedback. | Give designers and devs autonomy to propose solutions. | Document design decisions clearly. |
| **Develop**    | Write clean, minimal code; avoid unnecessary complexity. | Build features that deliver immediate value. | Measure progress with working increments. | Empower devs to self-organize around tasks. | Maintain visible boards and CI/CD pipelines. |
| **Deploy**     | Streamline release process; automate where possible. | Ensure deployments are stable and usable. | Track release metrics and user adoption. | Allow ops/dev teams to own deployment strategy. | Communicate release notes and changes openly. |
| **Deliver**    | Provide value without over-engineering. | Ensure delivered features solve real problems. | Gather empirical feedback from users. | Empower support teams to act on feedback. | Keep delivery outcomes visible to stakeholders. |
| **Decommission** | Retire systems simply; avoid drawn-out shutdowns. | Ensure migration paths are workable for users. | Use data to decide when retirement is justified. | Empower teams to plan transitions responsibly. | Be transparent about timelines and impacts. |

---

## Key Takeaway
- **7D Agile** gives structure: phases from *Discover → Decommission*.  
- **SWEET principles** guide behavior: *Simplicity, Workable, Empirical, Empowering, Transparent*.  
- Together, they ensure Agile stays both **disciplined and adaptive**.

- **Team size: 9 total**
- **3 leaders** (Alpha, Beta, Gamma)
- **6 execution agents** (2 per leader)
- **Reporting cadence**: agents report mission completion to leaders; leaders report consolidated status at each 7D checkpoint.
